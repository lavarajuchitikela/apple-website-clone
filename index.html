The Global Fund
provides access to
critical HIV prevention,
testing, education and
treatment services.

Apple-supported grants
have provided life-saving
treatment to millions of
people living with HIV.

Here's how Apple and (RED)
are partnering to save lives.

Every Apple (PRODUCT)RED
purchase contributes to
the Global Fund’s fight against
AIDS and COVID‑19.

The Global Fund uses 100% of
Apple’s contribution to finance HIV/AIDS
programmes and to reduce the impact of
COVID‑19 on these programmes.

Every dollar raised supports
communities that are
affected by AIDS across
sub‑Saharan Africa.
